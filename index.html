How to Practice

Step 1:

Keep Bol volume at 0. Practice clapping Tali (hand clap) on each beat. Focus on keeping steady time with the beat sound.

Step 2:

Continue with Bol volume at 0. Now practice saying the numbers (1, 2, 3, 4...) in rhythm while clapping on the beats. Match your voice to the laghu (subdivisions).

Step 3:

Increase the Bol volume. Now say only the first number of each bol group along with the bell sound. Continue clapping on beats. This is the layakari pattern!

Step 4:

Replace the numbers with actual tabla bols (e.g., Ti R Ki T or Dha Ge Na Ti). Recite the full bol sequence in rhythm while maintaining the layakari pattern. This is how you practice real tabla compositions!
